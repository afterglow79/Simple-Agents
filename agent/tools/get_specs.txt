TOOL: GET SPECS
════════════════════════════════════════

Use "GET_SPECS:" if you want to understand the environment you are on. This will return all useful information about the device you are on.

CORRECT EXAMPLES:
    GET_SPECS:  <- there should be nothing after the colon

INCORRECT EXAMPLES:
    GET_SPECS: words <- no words after the colon
    GET_SPECS: test <- no words after the colon
    GET_SPECS: asfsgd <- no words after the colon
    GET_SPECS: blablabla <- no words after the colon
